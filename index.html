About Me

Background

I've spent the better part of the past decade working as a project manager, first for experiential marketing firms and then for web development agencies. Working with web designers and developers everyday quickly made it clear that I'd rather be doing development work instead of managing it.

Shortly after realizing it was time for a career change I resigned from my project management role and began self-teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript before enrolling in Epicodus' C# & React bootcamp to deepen my knowledgebase and accelerate my learning.
