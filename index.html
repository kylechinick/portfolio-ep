About Me

Background

I've spent the better part of the past decade working as a project manager, first for epxeriential marketing firms and then for web development agencies. Working with web designers and developers every day quickly made it clear that I'd rather be doing development work instead of managing it.

Shortly after realizing it was time for a career change I resigned from my project management role and began self-teaching myself HTML and CSS before enrolling in Epicodus' C# & React bootcamp to deepen my knowledgebase and accelerate my learning.

Interests, Hobbies, and Skills

Part of my interest in development as a career comes from my lifelong interst in learning — something that the development industry provides infinite opportiunities for.

In my freetime you' likely find me doing the following:

  1. Working through programming tutorials
  2. Tinkering with my own personal projects
  3. Spending time with my wife and dog
  4. Lifting weights (and setting them back down!)
  5. Playing old video games for the 9000th time
